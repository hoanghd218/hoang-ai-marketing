ENTERING DATA FOR GOLD TOP PERFORMERS
Next, from your references, identify and document the Gold top-
performing content.
On the GSB sheet:
Enter the video title in the VIDEO column under the Gold
section, and hyperlink it using Command + K (Control + K on
Windows).
Record the page’s name in the PAGE column.
Note the number of views in the VIEWS column.
Enter the number of followers/subscribers in the
FOLLOWERS/SUBS column.
