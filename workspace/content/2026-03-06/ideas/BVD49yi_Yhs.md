## Buoc 2: Knowledge Nuggets

### Nugget 1 -- Paradigm Shift
**Tieu de:** Benchmark cao khong co nghia la model tot hon trong thuc te
**Mo ta:** OpenAI cong bo benchmark GPT 5.4 vuot Opus 4.6, nhung khi test thuc te (viet, giai thich, phan tich), Opus thang 3/4 hang muc. Con so tren giay khong bang trai nghiem that.

### Nugget 2 -- Bai hoc thuc te / So sanh
**Tieu de:** GPT 5.4 viet dai khi can ngan, viet ngan khi can dai
**Mo ta:** GPT 5.4 viet fiction dai dong du yeu cau "be concise", nhung tra loi cau hoi dia chinh tri qua ngan du can chi tiet. Day la loi calibration nghiem trong ve do dai phan hoi.

### Nugget 3 -- Principle
**Tieu de:** Biet khi nao ngan gon, khi nao chi tiet -- dau hieu cua tri thong minh that
**Mo ta:** Opus tu dong phan bo do dai phu hop: ngan khi can ngan, dai khi can giai thich ky. Kha nang nay la mot tin hieu tri tue thuc su ma benchmark khong do duoc.

### Nugget 4 -- Framework
**Tieu de:** Framework danh gia AI model: 4 chieu thuc te
**Mo ta:** Test model qua 4 chieu: (1) Viet sang tao, (2) Giai thich/day, (3) Phan tich/lap luan, (4) Visual reasoning. Cho buc tranh toan canh hon la chi nhin benchmark.

### Nugget 5 -- Canh bao
**Tieu de:** Dung tin AI dem calo tu hinh anh
**Mo ta:** Ca GPT 5.4 va Opus 4.6 deu sai lech lon khi uoc luong calo tu anh chup mon an (sai 270 va 420 calo). Khong nen dung AI vision de theo doi calo.

### Nugget 6 -- Principle
**Tieu de:** Suy nghi lau hon khong co nghia la tra loi tot hon
**Mo ta:** GPT 5.4 mat gap 10 lan thoi gian suy nghi cho cau hoi dia chinh tri nhung cho ra cau tra loi ne tranh, khong quyet doan. Nhieu compute khong dong nghia voi chat luong.

### Nugget 7 -- Canh bao
**Tieu de:** Ban dang cho AI nhung viec qua de
**Mo ta:** Model hien tai (Opus 4.6, GPT 5.4) manh hon nhieu so voi nhung gi da so nguoi dung yeu cau. Nhieu nguoi van mac ket o tu duy 2024-2025, chi giao viec don gian.

### Nugget 8 -- Framework
**Tieu de:** Chon model theo loai cong viec
**Mo ta:** GPT 5.4 manh ve coding, debugging, visual reasoning. Opus 4.6 manh ve viet sang tao, giai thich, day hoc, lam theo chi dan. Chon model dua tren use case chinh cua ban.
