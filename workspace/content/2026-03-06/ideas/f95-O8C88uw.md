## Buoc 1: Tom tat noi dung chinh

Video huong dan 5 cap do su dung Claude Co-work danh cho nguoi khong biet code. Tac gia (Brock) trinh bay tu cap co ban nhat (Import -- chuyen du lieu tu ChatGPT sang Claude) den cao nhat (Automation -- lap lich task tu dong). Gia tri cot loi: xay dung he thong context files (goals.md, company.md, glossary.md) de Claude hieu doanh nghiep cua ban, sau do ket noi apps (Gmail, Notion, Slack), tao workflow bang plugin, va tu dong hoa bang scheduled tasks. Thong diep chinh: dung AI khong chi la chat -- ma la xay mot he thong lam viec tu dong.

## Buoc 2: Knowledge Nuggets

### Nugget 1 -- Framework: 5 Cap do Claude Co-work
- Import > Foundation > Workflows > Ecosystem > Automation
- Moi cap xay tren cap truoc. Da so nguoi dung chi o cap 1 (chat don thuan).
- Goc khai thac: Infographic "5 tang cua AI Workflow" hoac short video "Ban dang o cap may?"

### Nugget 2 -- Paradigm Shift: Onboard AI nhu onboard nhan vien moi
- Thay vi "cau hinh tool", hay "dao tao nhan vien": cho AI doc muc tieu, thong tin cong ty, thuat ngu noi bo.
- Goc khai thac: Facebook post "Sai lam lon nhat khi dung AI" -- doi chieu cach dung cu vs cach dung moi.

### Nugget 3 -- Framework: Claude.md Kitchen Bible Pattern
- claude.md la tap chi dan tu cap nhat. Sau moi phien lam viec, he thong tu viet lai huong dan cua chinh no.
- Goc khai thac: Short video demo thuc te -- cho xem file claude.md thay doi sau 1 tuan.

### Nugget 4 -- Step-by-step: Chuyen tri nho tu ChatGPT sang Claude trong 60 giay
- Dung prompt co san cua Anthropic, paste vao ChatGPT, copy ket qua, paste nguoc vao Claude.
- Goc khai thac: Short video tutorial "1 phut chuyen nha tu ChatGPT sang Claude".

### Nugget 5 -- Canh bao: Khong co Foundation = chatbot binh thuong
- Neu nhay thang vao prompting ma khong setup context files (goals, company, glossary), Claude tra loi chung chung nhu moi chatbot khac.
- Goc khai thac: Facebook post "Tai sao AI cua ban cu tra loi nhu robot?"

### Nugget 6 -- Framework: Plugin-to-Schedule workflow promotion
- Xay workflow thu cong truoc, test cho chac, roi moi chuyen thanh scheduled task.
- Goc khai thac: Actionable post "Quy tac vang: Thu cong truoc, tu dong sau."

### Nugget 7 -- Case study: Dashboard tu dong moi sang 8h
- Moi sang he thong scrape competitor YouTube, tao HTML dashboard, gui qua Slack.
- Goc khai thac: Short video "Moi sang minh nhan duoc bao cao nay tu AI" -- show dashboard.

### Nugget 8 -- Warning: Scheduled tasks can loi laptop mo va Claude Desktop chay
- Khac voi cloud automation (Zapier, Make), Claude Co-work chay local. Tat may = khong chay.
- Goc khai thac: Facebook post canh bao "Dieu khong ai noi ve Claude Automation".
