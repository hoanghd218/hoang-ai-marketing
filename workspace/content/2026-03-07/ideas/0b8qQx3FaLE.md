--- KNOWLEDGE NUGGETS ---

1. [Framework] - Quy trinh "Reverse-Engineering" phong cach creator bang Manus AI
   Dua video cua creator ban thich vao Manus AI, no se tu phan tich phong cach, typography, am nhac, cau truc kich ban -- tao ra mot "style guide" (so tay phong cach) day du. Giong nhu thue mot chuyen gia phan tich phong cach cho ban.

2. [Framework] - He thong 2 Workflow cho 90% video
   Chi can 2 thao tac AI chinh: (1) Nang cap background bang AI image gen -- lay 1 khung hinh tinh, them do vat, anh sang qua Freepik/Nana Banana Pro; (2) Tao transition bang AI video gen -- lay khung hinh dau + khung hinh cuoi, de AI tao doan chuyen canh 3-4 giay. 90% nhung canh dep nhat cua Kova deu dung 2 cach nay.

3. [Paradigm Shift] - Phong ngu co the thay studio Hollywood
   Kova lam tat ca video trong phong ky tuc xa dai hoc. AI giup them den, ke sach, cua so, hoa -- bien phong trong thanh set quay chuyen nghiep. Khong can studio, khong can tien.

4. [Principle] - Prompt don gian cho ket qua tot hon
   Kova dung prompt cuc ngan nhu "more flowers" hay "orange tulips in a vase". Ly do: khi prompt don gian, model AI se chon ket qua ma no lam tot nhat, thay vi co ep theo yeu cau qua chi tiet.

5. [Canh bao] - Khong dung tu phu dinh trong prompt video
   Khi tao video bang AI, khong viet "camera khong di chuyen" ma viet "camera is static" (camera dung yen). Model AI khong hieu "khong" -- no doc tung tu roi lam theo keyword, khong phan biet khang dinh hay phu dinh.

6. [Framework] - Cau truc 5 man cho video ngan
   Hook (mo) > Conflict (xung dot/thach thuc) > Build (qua trinh lam) > Problem & Resolution (van de va giai quyet) > CTA (keu goi hanh dong). Cau truc nay giong hanh trinh nguoi hung, giup giu nguoi xem tu dau den cuoi.

7. [Principle] - Moi video can 1 cau khai niem vua ky thuat vua cam xuc
   Truoc khi quay bat ky thu gi, viet mot cau duy nhat giai thich y tuong. Cau do phai vua co yeu to ky thuat (lam gi), vua co yeu to cam xuc (tai sao nguoi xem quan tam). Vi du: "Flash drive ma nhan vat beo len khi ban them file."

8. [Framework] - He thong Obsidian + Claude Code cho content creator
   Kova dung Obsidian de luu tat ca template, kich ban, storyboard. Sau do dung Claude Code/Cursor de tu dong chuyen kich ban thanh storyboard theo template co san, tai cau truc thu muc, va thuc hien cac tac vu lap di lap lai. System (he thong) la dieu kien tien quyet de scale (mo rong) content.
