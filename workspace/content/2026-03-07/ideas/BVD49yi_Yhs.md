--- KNOWLEDGE NUGGETS ---

1. [Paradigm Shift] - Benchmark khong phai la tat ca
   GPT 5.4 thang Opus tren hau het benchmark, nhung khi test thuc te (viet truyen, giai thich, tra loi chinh tri), Opus thang 3/4 bai. Benchmark chi la ly thuyet, su dung hang ngay moi la thuc te.

2. [Diagnosis] - GPT 5.4 bi "dao nguoc do dai" -- viet dai khi can ngan, viet ngan khi can chi tiet
   Khi yeu cau "be concise" viet truyen, GPT 5.4 viet dai. Khi can giai thich chi tiet mot khai niem, no lai viet qua ngan. Day la diem yeu nghiem trong ve kha nang hieu ngon canh.

3. [Principle] - Biet khi nao ngan, khi nao dai la dau hieu cua tri thong minh thuc su
   Opus tu dong phan bo do dai phu hop: ngan khi viet fiction, dai hon khi giai thich ky thuat. Day la kha nang ma con nguoi gioi cung can co -- biet khi nao can di thang vao de, khi nao can giai thich ky.

4. [Framework] - Mo hinh danh gia AI 4 chieu: Writing, Teaching, Opinion, Vision
   David test 4 khia canh: (1) Viet truyen sang tao, (2) Giai thich khai niem, (3) Tra loi y kien/chinh tri, (4) Nhan dien hinh anh. Day la cach danh gia toan dien hon la chi nhin benchmark.

5. [Canh bao] - Dung tin AI de dem calo tu hinh anh
   Ca hai model deu sai lech lon khi uoc tinh calo tu anh (Opus sai 420 calo, GPT 5.4 sai 270 calo so voi thuc te 500 calo). Ai dung AI de track calo se bi sai nghiem trong.

6. [Canh bao] - Moi nguoi dang cho AI lam viec qua de
   David noi nhieu nguoi van o tu duy 2024-2025, cho AI lam nhung viec don gian. Cac model hien tai (Opus 4.6, GPT 5.4) manh hon rat nhieu -- can giao viec kho hon, du an tham vong hon.

7. [Framework] - Context engineering voi XML wrapping de tom tat tot hon
   David dung ky thuat boc noi dung trong the XML (<text>...</text>) truoc khi yeu cau model tom tat. Day la pro tip giup model hieu ro dau la noi dung can xu ly, dau la chi dan.

8. [Paradigm Shift] - Nganh bi AI anh huong nhieu nhat cung la nganh co loi nhuan cao nhat
   Bieu do tu Anthropic cho thay: nganh software, IT, tai chinh bi AI anh huong manh nhat -- nhung cung la noi co co hoi lon nhat. Nganh an toan hon (xay dung, van tai) thi it co hoi tang truong dot pha.
