--- KNOWLEDGE NUGGETS ---

1. [Paradigm Shift] - Diffusion thay the Autoregression cho viec tao van ban
   Cac mo hinh AI hien tai (GPT, Claude, Gemini) tao van ban tung tu mot, tu trai sang phai, khong the quay lai sua. Mercury 2 dung kien truc diffusion -- tao toan bo cau tra loi cung luc roi tinh chinh dan -- giong cach mot nguoi viet nhap roi chinh sua, thay vi viet mot chieu khong duoc xoa.

2. [Diagnosis] - Loi chong chat (Error Compounding) la nguyen nhan goc cua hallucination
   Khi mo hinh AI viet tung tu mot, neu tu dau sai thi moi tu sau deu xay tren cai sai do. Cang viet dai cang sai nhieu. Day la ly do AI hay "ao giac" -- va Mercury 2 giai quyet bang cach cho phep sua bat ky tu nao trong toan bo cau tra loi.

3. [Framework] - Chu ky 5 nam tu kien truc moi den ung dung dai trang
   Paper Transformer ra doi nam 2017, nhung phai den 2022 (ChatGPT) moi thay doi the gioi -- mat 5 nam. Neu diffusion model la buoc tiep theo, chung ta co the dang o giai doan dau cua mot chu ky tuong tu.

4. [So lieu / So sanh] - Nhanh gap 5-10 lan, gia re cuc ky
   Mercury 2 tao hon 1,000 token/giay, nhanh gap 5-10 lan so voi cac mo hinh tuong duong (Claude 4.5 Haiku, GPT-5 Mini). Gia chi $0.25/trieu token dau vao va $0.75/trieu token dau ra -- re hon nhieu so voi cac mo hinh lon.

5. [Step-by-step / Huong dan] - Dung Mercury 2 ngay qua OpenRouter hoac API truc tiep
   Mercury 2 tuong thich API cua OpenAI -- chi can doi 1 dong code (thay endpoint) la chuyen tu GPT/Claude sang Mercury 2. Co the dung qua OpenRouter, qua chat.inceptionlabs.ai, hoac API truc tiep. Ho tro tool use, structured output, RAG, 128k context window.

6. [Paradigm Shift] - Toc do chi la "bonus" -- kha nang tu sua loi moi la dot pha chinh
   Nhieu nguoi tap trung vao viec Mercury 2 nhanh, nhung gia tri thuc su la no co the quay lai sua bat ky phan nao trong cau tra loi. Dieu nay co the giai quyet van de hallucination va giup AI lap ke hoach phuc tap hon -- dieu ma Yann LeCun da noi cac mo hinh autoregressive khong the lam tot.

7. [Canh bao] - Dung nghi kien truc hien tai la vinh vien
   Truoc Transformer, ai cung dung RNN va nghi do la tuong lai. Transformer thay doi tat ca. Bay gio Transformer dang thong tri -- nhung diffusion co the la buoc ngoat tiep theo. Nguoi khong theo doi se bi bo lai phia sau.
