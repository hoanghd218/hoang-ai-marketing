--- TOM TAT ---

Video gioi thieu tinh nang Loop moi cua Claude Code -- cho phep dat lich nhac nho va lap lai tac vu tu dong trong phien lam viec, toi da 3 ngay. Tac gia Nate Herk demo truc tiep cach tao reminder, dat interval kiem tra ClickUp moi 10 phut, va so sanh su khac biet giua Loop (tam thoi, trong phien) va Scheduled Tasks (lau dai, co catch-up). Doi tuong la developer va nguoi dung Claude Code muon tu dong hoa cong viec hang ngay. Gia tri cot loi: giup nguoi dung hieu khi nao dung Loop (viec ngan han, trong phien), khi nao dung Scheduled Tasks (viec dai han, lap lai hang ngay/tuan), va cac luu y quan trong khi su dung.

--- KNOWLEDGE NUGGETS ---

1. [Framework] - Loop vs. Scheduled Tasks: Khi nao dung cai nao?
   Loop danh cho viec ngan han, trong 1 phien lam viec (toi da 3 ngay). Scheduled Tasks danh cho viec dai han, lap lai hang ngay/tuan/thang. Cau hoi quyet dinh: "Ban can giup NGAY BAY GIO hay can giup MOI NGAY?"

2. [Framework] - Ba cong cu Cron co ban: Create, List, Delete
   Claude Code dung 3 cong cu cron de quan ly loop: cron create (tao lich), cron list (xem danh sach), cron delete (huy). Tat ca deu goi duoc bang ngon ngu tu nhien, khong can nho lenh.

3. [Paradigm Shift] - Claude Code tu dong lam viec ma khong can ban bam nut
   Claude Code khong con chi la cong cu "hoi-dap". Voi Loop, no tu dong thuc day, kiem tra, bao cao -- giong nhu mot nhan vien tu giac lam viec theo lich ma ban dat.

4. [Canh bao] - Context Rot khi Loop chay tan suat cao
   Neu loop chay moi 10 phut va moi lan tra ve bao cao dai, context se nhanh chong bi "rot" (giam chat luong) vi qua nhieu token tich luy. Can giu output cua moi lan loop ngan gon.

5. [Canh bao] - Dong phien = Mat tat ca Loop
   Loop gan voi phien lam viec. Dong tab VS Code, dong terminal, hoac dong desktop app = tat ca loop bi huy. Khong co catch-up, khong co luu tru.

6. [Nguyen tac] - Loop co han su dung 3 ngay
   Moi loop tu dong het han sau 3 ngay de tranh quen tat. Neu can lau hon, nen chuyen sang Scheduled Tasks hoac tao lai loop moi.

7. [Nguyen tac] - Moi phien lam viec doc lap, loop khong chia se
   Tab 1 va Tab 2 la 2 phien khac nhau. Loop tao o Tab 1 khong hien o Tab 2. Khong co loop "toan cuc" chay xuyen phien.

8. [Huong dan] - Cac truong hop su dung Loop thuc te
   Cho email khan cap (kiem tra moi 5 phut), theo doi deploy (moi gio), sprint 3 ngay (kiem tra tien do lien tuc), theo doi log, chay skill tu dong theo lich. Loop co the goi bat ky skill nao cua Claude Code.
