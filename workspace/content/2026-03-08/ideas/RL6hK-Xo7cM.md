--- KNOWLEDGE NUGGETS ---

1. [Paradigm Shift] - Claude khong phai cong cu hoi dap, ma la cong cu chay quy trinh
   Hau het moi nguoi dung Claude nhu Google -- hoi mot cau, doc tra loi, roi di. Nhung voi Skills, Claude hieu cach ban lam viec, tu dong thuc hien ca mot quy trinh, va tra ve ket qua la file that (HTML, markdown) -- khong phai chi mot doan text.

2. [Framework] - Bo ba suc manh: Skills + Connectors + Scheduled Tasks
   Skills (ky nang) day Claude biet lam gi. Connectors (cau noi) giup Claude truy cap email, lich, Notion, Zapier. Scheduled Tasks (lich hen) giup Claude tu dong chay ma khong can ban ra lenh. Ba thu nay gop lai = mot nhan vien AI chay tu dong 24/7.

3. [Framework] - Morning Briefing: gom thong tin tu 5 app thanh 1 trang web moi sang
   Thay vi mo email, lich, Slack, to-do, tin tuc rieng le -- mot lenh duy nhat de Claude quet tat ca va tao ra mot trang web dep, co cau truc: lich hop, email can xu ly, tin AI moi, va 3 uu tien hang dau trong ngay.

4. [Principle] - Output phai la "deliverable" (san pham giao duoc), khong phai "response" (cau tra loi)
   Skill Research Assistant khong chi tra loi cau hoi ma tao ra mot tai lieu co cau truc: co tom tat, co nguon trich dan, co so lieu -- luu thanh file that, chia se duoc ngay cho team. Day la su khac biet giua dung AI nhu chatbot va dung AI nhu nhan vien.

5. [Diagnosis] - Ly do cuoc hop cam thay vo nghia: khong ai rut ra duoc viec can lam
   Skill Meeting Notes to Action Items bien ban ghi cuoc hop thanh: tom tat ngan, danh sach viec can lam cu the cho tung nguoi, deadline ro rang. Mot cuoc hop 1 tieng thanh 10 buoc hanh dong cu the.

6. [Step-by-step] - Tao slide trinh bay chi tu mot cau noi
   Skill Slide Deck Generator nhan mot cau mo ta chu de va tu dong tao bai trinh bay HTML co hieu ung chuyen dong, hinh anh, thiet ke dep -- tiet kiem trung binh 3 gio lam slide thu cong.

7. [Paradigm Shift] - Meta-skill: Skill tao ra skill
   Skill thu 7 la Skill Creator -- cho phep bat ky ai tao skill rieng cho cong viec cua minh. Chi can noi chuyen voi Claude, mo ta quy trinh ban lam hang ngay, va no se dong goi thanh mot skill hoan chinh. Bat cu viec gi ban lam di lam lai deu la ung vien de thanh skill.

8. [Warning] - Skills khong co Connectors chi dung lai o muc copy-paste
   Neu khong ket noi Claude voi cac app (email, lich, Notion...) qua connectors, ban van phai tu copy ket qua va dan vao cho khac. Connectors bien skill tu "cong cu tra loi" thanh "nhan vien tu dong lam viec".
