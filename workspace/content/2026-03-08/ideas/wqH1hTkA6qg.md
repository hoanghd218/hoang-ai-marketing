--- KNOWLEDGE NUGGETS ---

1. [Paradigm Shift] - Ngung xay agent, hay xay skill
   Anthropic -- cong ty tao ra Claude -- khuyen: tuong lai khong phai xay nhieu agent rieng le, ma la xay nhieu skill cho mot agent duy nhat. Mot agent + nhieu skill = lam duoc moi viec.

2. [Framework] - Cau truc skill file: Markdown + Scripts + References
   Mot skill gom: file markdown (huong dan), scripts Python (Claude chay duoc), templates, tai lieu tham khao. Don gian nhat chi can 1 file markdown viet trong 5 phut.

3. [Principle] - Progressive Disclosure: AI chi doc khi can
   Claude khong nap tat ca skill vao bo nho cung luc. No chi doc tieu de (nhu doc gay sach), khi can moi mo ra doc chi tiet. Nho vay co the co hang tram skill ma khong bi qua tai.

4. [Warning] - Xay agent rieng cho moi viec = khong scale duoc
   Moi agent co tool rieng, setup rieng, kien truc rieng. Lam nhu vay met moi va khong mo rong duoc. Day la sai lam pho bien.

5. [Diagnosis] - Intelligence khong phai la expertise
   AI agent hien tai rat thong minh nhung khong co chuyen mon cu the. Giong nhu thue thien tai chua lam thue bao gio -- thong minh nhung khong hieu biet.

6. [Framework] - MCP la doi tay, Skills la kinh nghiem
   MCP (Model Context Protocol) giup AI ket noi voi cong cu ben ngoai. Skills day AI biet phai lam gi voi du lieu do. Ket hop ca hai = agent vua lam duoc, vua biet cach lam.

7. [Paradigm Shift] - Nguoi khong biet code cung tuy chinh duoc AI
   Truoc day, tuy chinh AI can biet lap trinh. Voi skills, bat ky ai cung viet duoc huong dan bang tieng Anh binh thuong de day AI lam viec theo cach cua minh.

8. [Framework] - Sub-agent delegation: chia viec cho nhieu agent con
   Skill co the chi thi Claude Code chay nhieu sub-agent song song, moi agent lam mot phan viec cu the. Giong nhu nha thau chinh thue 5 nha thau phu de xay nha hieu qua hon.

Ban mat bao lau de giai thich cho AI moi lan ban can no lam gi do?

5 phut? 10 phut? Roi lan sau lai giai thich lai tu dau?

Co cach lam mot lan, AI nho mai.

Anthropic goi no la "Skill" -- ban viet mot file huong dan ngan, AI doc xong la biet cach lam. Giong nhu ban viet so tay cho nhan vien moi vay.

Day la cach lam:

Buoc 1: Tao mot file markdown (.md)
Viet bang tieng Anh binh thuong. Khong can code. Chi can ghi ro: "Khi co nguoi yeu cau viec nay, hay lam theo cac buoc sau..."

Buoc 2: Liet ke cac buoc cu the
Vi du ban muon AI viet blog post cho cong ty. Ghi ro: dung giong van nay, cau truc bai viet gom nhung phan nay, luon ket thuc bang CTA nhu the nay.

Buoc 3: Them tai lieu tham khao (neu can)
Bo them file mau, template, vi du cu the vao cung thu muc. AI se doc va hoc theo.

Buoc 4: Luu vao dung thu muc
Voi Claude Code, luu vao thu muc skills. Xong. Tu gio AI tu biet khi nao can dung skill nao.

Dieu hay nhat: AI khong nap het tat ca skill vao dau cung luc. No chi doc tieu de, giong nhu ban luot qua gay sach tren ke. Khi can quyen nao, no moi rut ra doc. Nho vay ban co the co hang tram skill ma AI khong bi "qua tai".

Ket qua: Thay vi mat 10 phut giai thich moi lan, ban chi mat 5 phut mot lan duy nhat. AI tu hieu va lam dung tu lan sau.

Mot nguoi. Mot he thong. Van hanh nhu ca team.

Ban muon bat dau voi skill nao? Comment cho minh biet -- minh se goi y skill phu hop voi cong viec cua ban.

CONTENT #3: "MCP la doi tay. Skills la kinh nghiem."
Format: Facebook Post (Text + Hinh)
Nugget goc: #6 (Framework MCP + Skills) + #8 (Sub-agent delegation)
=======================================

Cho AI ket noi voi 100 cong cu ma khong biet cach dung = vo nghia.

Giong nhu ban mua cho nhan vien moi day du dung cu -- may tinh, phan mem, tai khoan -- nhung khong dao tao gi het. Ho co dung cu nhung khong biet lam gi voi no.

Day chinh la van de cua MCP (Model Context Protocol -- cau noi giup AI noi chuyen voi cac app khac).

MCP giup AI ket noi duoc voi moi thu: Google Sheets, Notion, API, database. Nhung chi la "ket noi". AI co doi tay roi, nhung chua co kinh nghiem.

Skills la phan con thieu. Skills day AI: "Khi lay du lieu tu Google Sheets, hay phan tich theo cach nay, trinh bay theo format nay, va gui ket qua vao Notion theo cau truc nay."

MCP = doi tay. Skills = kinh nghiem.

Ket hop ca hai, ban co mot AI vua voi duoc toi moi noi, vua biet lam gi khi toi noi.

Va dieu dien ro hon: mot skill co the chi huy nhieu agent con chay song song. Giong nhu nha thau chinh thue 5 doi tho cung lam mot luc -- nguoi lam dien, nguoi lam nuoc, nguoi lam mong. Nhanh gap 5 lan.

AI khong thay the ban. AI nhan ban ban.
