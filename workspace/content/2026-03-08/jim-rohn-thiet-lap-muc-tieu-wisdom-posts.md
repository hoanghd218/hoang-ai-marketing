NGUON: Jim Rohn — Thiet Lap Muc Tieu (Goal Setting Workshop)
CHU DE: Thiet lap muc tieu hieu qua, phat trien ban than
═══════════════════════════════════════

Wisdom Points:
1. Cau hoi la cong cu manh hon cau tra loi — chi bang cau hoi ban moi kham pha ra minh thuc su can gi
2. Viet ra 5 dieu tu hao — nhin lai thanh qua de tao dong luc
3. Lap danh sach 40-50 muc tieu cho 10 nam toi
4. "Khong co danh sach muc tieu = tai khoan ngan hang rong tui"
5. Gan thoi han 1-3-5-7-10 nam cho moi muc tieu
6. Danh dau tich va an mung khi hoan thanh
7. Chon 4 uu tien quan trong nhat cho nam dau tien
8. Tim ly do TAI SAO du lon — cach thuc hien se de dang
9. Con nguoi ban tro thanh quan trong hon nhung gi ban co
10. Hai ngay quan trong nhat: ngay sinh ra va ngay biet minh sinh ra de lam gi
