--- TOM TAT ---

Video huong dan cach su dung Claude Skills 2.0 -- phien ban moi co tich hop tinh nang eval (kiem thu tu dong) giup toi uu skill nhanh hon. Tac gia trinh bay quy trinh 3 buoc: tao skill bang prompt co cau truc, chay eval de do luong chat luong output theo tieu chi cu the, roi dung ket qua de lap lai va cai thien. Doi tuong huong den la nguoi dung Claude Code/Claude Co-work muon xay dung automation cho cong viec hang ngay.

--- KNOWLEDGE NUGGETS ---

1. [Framework] - Skill Creation Prompt Framework
   Quy trinh tao skill gom: ten + trigger, muc tieu chinh, connectors (ket noi), reference files (tai lieu tham khao), quy trinh tung buoc, va phan progressive updates (tu cap nhat). Day la khung de tao skill tot ngay tu phien ban dau.

2. [Framework] - Eval-Driven Skill Iteration Loop
   Sau khi tao skill, chay eval voi tieu chi cu the (vi du: style match, word count, personal stories). Xem ket qua, them feedback, roi de Claude tu dong cap nhat skill. Lap lai 5-10 lan de dat skill tot.

3. [Paradigm Shift] - Xay skill khong kho -- lap lai moi kho
   Phan lon nguoi dung dung lai o phien ban dau tien. Nhung skill tot can 5-10 lan lap lai. Viec tao skill chi la buoc bat dau, iteration (lap lai cai thien) moi la buoc quyet dinh chat luong.

4. [Canh bao] - Dung de Claude tu dinh nghia tieu chi eval
   Khi khong chi dinh tieu chi cu the, Claude tu nghi ra tieu chi -- thuong khong sat voi nhu cau that. Phai tu dinh nghia ro rang optimize cho gi va do bang gi.

5. [Nguyen tac] - Toi uu tung bien mot
   Khong nen test 5-6 thu cung luc vi qua nhieu bien so. Moi lan chay eval, chi tap trung toi uu 1 thu duy nhat de thay ro ket qua.

6. [Framework] - A/B Testing cho Skill Optimization
   Tao 2+ phien ban skill, chay cung 1 input, so sanh output. Phu hop khi da co skill hoat dong tot va muon nang cap them -- vi du toi uu toc do, token usage, hoac chat luong output.

7. [Paradigm Shift] - Context engineering qua A/B test reference files
   Thay vi doan, co the dung A/B test de thu skill co va khong co tung reference file cu the. Vi du: thu bo file "voice personality" de xem no thuc su giup hay hai output.

8. [Nguyen tac] - Skill co the loi thoi khi model nang cap
   Khi model moi ra (vi du Opus 4.7), skill cu co the khong con can thiet hoac can cap nhat lai. Nen chay A/B test so sanh skill voi non-skill de kiem tra.
